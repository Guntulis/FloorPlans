Welcome to Planner 5D

An easy-to-use home builder that uses drag-and-drops functions. Build walls, drop furniture, edit sizes and colors, add design details and more. When you’re finished, you can create a realistic rendering of your interior and exterior!

Walls & Rooms

Change the size and layout of your rooms by adjusting the walls. You can move walls, split them into sections or make them invisible. To change the wall placement, use the colored dots to drag the walls and rearrange, or connect to other dots to merge walls.

Catalog

There are over 4k items in the Planner 5D Catalog! They are grouped by category, with many options for each type of item. Add items to your project by selecting, dragging and dropping.

Selecting colors & materials

Play around with various designs as you change the colors and materials for any item! Select the item, click on the editing setting (the paint roller icon) and choose a part of the item that you want to edit. Select the texture, material and color to make the item unique.

2D & 3D Modes

Switch between 2D and 3D for a smooth design process. With 2D mode, create your floor plan and drop furniture. With 3D mode, explore the flow of your space and finalize design details.
